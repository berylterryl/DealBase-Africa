Frequently asked questions

How is this different from other African deal databases?
DealFlow Africa is curated by a professional finance team — not AI-scraped. Every transaction is verified, summarised, and contextualised by experienced analysts at Achievers Regal. You also get direct access to the people behind the data.
How often is the database updated?
The database is updated daily. New transactions are added as they are announced and verified. Full access subscribers receive weekly deal alert emails every Friday.
What types of deals are tracked?
We track M&A (mergers and acquisitions), private equity investments, venture capital rounds, infrastructure deals, and select debt financing transactions across all African markets.
Can I request data on a specific sector or country?
Yes. Full access and advisory clients can request custom research cuts, sector-specific reports, and targeted deal sourcing. Contact the team to discuss your specific needs.
How do I get access?
Fill in the contact form and the Achievers Regal team will be in touch within 24 hours to discuss access options and pricing tailored to your needs.
